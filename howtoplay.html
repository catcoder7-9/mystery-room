How to Play

Mystery Room — Detective's Handbook

🔄
Puzzle Type 01
Word Scramble
The letters of a word have been jumbled up. Unscramble them to reveal the hidden word or phrase.
Example
TEMHATNNA → MANHATTAN
3️⃣
Puzzle Type 02
Every 3rd Letter
A secret message is hidden inside a block of text. Isolate every third letter to reveal what is written. There are always three.
Example
XYCABRATKPTMNSS → CATS
🔐
Puzzle Type 03
Caesar Cipher
Every letter has been shifted forward in the alphabet by three places. To decode it, shift each letter back by three. There are always three.
Example — shift back by 3
KHOOR → HELLO
A B C D E F G H ...
D E F G H I J K ...
🔀
Puzzle Type 04
Anagram
All the letters are there — but rearranged. Rearrange every letter to spell out a new word or phrase.
Example
SILENT → LISTEN
LISTEN → ENLIST
⚖️ Rules of the Room
🕯️You have 3 lives per case. Lose them all and the case goes cold.
💡You can request a hint — but it costs one life.
⏱️You have 20 minutes per case. The timer starts when you begin.
🔒Cases are locked. Solve each one to unlock the next.
🔍The final clue in every case always reveals the killer's name.
🎯Look out for Jennifer Lynn Barnes references hidden throughout.
