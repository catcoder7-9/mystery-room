Case Briefing — Detective Eyes Only
Three days into a transatlantic crossing aboard the luxury cruise ship SS Meridian, Eleanor Voss, 54, the world's bestselling crime novelist, has been found dead in her private cabin. The door was locked. Her manuscript — the one she had been writing in secret for two years — is missing.

The ship is four days from port. No one can leave. The killer is still on board.

Four passengers were seen near her cabin that evening.

Cecile Voss — Eleanor's younger sister. Cut out of Eleanor's will six months ago.
Dr. Paul Harte — Eleanor's personal physician. Travelling with her at her request.
Ivy Chen — A journalist. Claims she was writing a profile on Eleanor for a magazine.
Five clues are hidden aboard this ship. Find them all and name the killer before we reach port.
